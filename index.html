Jiani Cao (曹佳妮)

I am currently a fourth-year Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong (CityU). My Ph.D. supervisor is Prof. Zhenjiang Li. Before that, I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering, from Xidian University (XDU), China in 2020.

My research interests primarily lie in designing AI-powered IoT and mobile systems, with a focus on proposing innovative and efficient sensing techniques to enhance smart health applications.
